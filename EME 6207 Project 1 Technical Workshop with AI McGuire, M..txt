<h2>	Similarities and Differences Between Perplexity and ChatGPT	</h2>

	<h3>	Similarities	</h3>
<p>	- Both platform responses answer the prompt effectively.	</p>
<p>	- Both offer continuous research prompts/questions regarding the initial prompt near the end of each response.	</p>
<p>	- Both offer resources when prompted.	</p> 

	<h3>	Differences	</h3>
<p>	- Accuracy and correctness: Perplexity proved to be more accurate and correct when it came to credible resources. In my experience, ChatGPT populated a lot of broken links or unreliable resources.	</p> 
<p>	- Clarity and understandability: ChatGPT’s layout made the content clearer and more understandable by breaking up the information into smaller sections with icons and pictures. Perplexity didn’t chunk the content in any type of way; it simply computed and listed the information in a more technical layout.	</p> 
<p>	- Depth and completeness: Perplexity offers tabs at the top of the page that include reliable links and images connecting to the conversation. ChatGPT has proved that its depth and completeness fall short due to broken links or sources from different countries in an unrecognizable language.	</p>

Additionally, the opportunity to compare different GenAI platforms broadened my perspective on alternative AI tools, rather than relying on only one.	</p>
